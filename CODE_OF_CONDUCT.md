# PDPR Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

PDPR (Public Dark Pattern Registry) is a **public-interest ethical infrastructure project**.

We are committed to building a community that is:

- Respectful  
- Ethical  
- Evidence-driven  
- Safe  
- Inclusive  
- Constructive  

We pledge to create a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age  
- Gender Identity  
- Sexual Orientation  
- Disability  
- Physical Appearance  
- Race  
- Nationality  
- Religion  
- Technical Background  
- Level of Experience

## Ethical Conduct Principles

PDPR operates under an **Ethics-First Model**.

All contributors must respect:

- Human autonomy.
- Psychological safety.
- Informed consent.
- Educational responsibility.
- Non-exploitation.
- Harm minimization.

We do not allow content that enables:

- Manipulation engineering.
- Psychological exploitation.
- User deception.
- Coercive UX design.

---

## Scientific Integrity

PDPR requires:

- Evidence-based claims.
- Honest uncertainty reporting.
- Clear explanation of assumptions.
- Responsible interpretation.
- Bias awareness

No sensationalism.  
No exaggeration.  
No fear-mongering.

---

## Professional Conduct

All interactions must remain:

- Civil
- Professional
- Calm
- Thoughtful

Disagreement is allowed.  
Disrespect is not.

---

## Community Responsibility

Every contributor shares responsibility for:

- Maintaining ethical tone.
- Supporting newcomers.
- Protecting vulnerable users.
- Encouraging thoughtful discourse.

---

## Moderation & Enforcement

PDPR maintainers have the right to:

- Remove harmful content.
- Lock discussions.
- Issue warnings.
- Suspend accounts.
- Ban repeat offenders.

Actions will be taken based on:

- Severity
- Intent
- Impact
- Repetition
